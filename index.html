About YAM Stake Pool

Not gonna lie to you - there's no touching origin story here.

We didn't name our pool YAM because it saved our grandmother during the Great Depression, or because we had a spiritual awakening while farming root vegetables.

Truth is, we wanted something:

  • Short enough that even crypto-fried brains could remember it
  • Easy to say after a few beers at a Cardano meetup
  • That could stand for "Your ADA Matters" without sounding completely corny

So... YAM it is.

Instead of fake stats that insult your intelligence, here's what we actually offer:

  • A no-BS approach to running a stake pool
  • Regular maintenance and updates (because we actually care)
  • Transparent communication (when things go well AND when they don't)
  • A pool operator who won't disappear to "find themselves" in Bali with your delegation rewards

We're not going to promise you'll get rich quick. We're not going to claim we have "proprietary staking technology" or "AI-enhanced block production" or whatever made-up nonsense other pools are selling this week.

YAM: Your ADA Matters. That's it. That's the story.

Join us if you appreciate honesty, a sense of humor, and a stake pool that doesn't take itself too seriously (while still taking your delegation very seriously).
